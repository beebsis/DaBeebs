Welcome to Da beebs

Da Beebs is a place for people to come and enjoy, while they're working or studying. Or simply if they just want to come and relax with a good atmosphere.

I want to build a Community as a place for people to come and relax, meanwhile they're studying or working or if they just wish to come. I'm am currently still a student myself, and I've been wanting a study partner(s) for a while to improve my own studies but it's easier said than done. So for people who're less social or have less time due to circumstances, I would like to give them the chance of getting a virtual study place and study partner to interact with and increase theirs and my own learning and mood.

- Beebsis 2022

Goals

These are the goals I have set for myself and the Community I wish to create.

Atmosphere

I wish to create a nice and calm atmospheric place where people can concentrate on their work and enjoy the place.

Of course we'll have our chaotic moments from time to time.

Safe Place

A safe place for people to come and escape classmates, colleagues or just every day trouble. This isn't a venting place, but we'll listen and help if possible - but we are not professionals.

Learning

You'll see everyone learning in one way or another in here, some of us study together around the world, even if it's completely different subjects and timezones.

Acknowledgement

A lot of people don't feel acknowledged in their daily life, especially students. I wish to let them know, that they're acknowledged by us, and give them a place to enjoy and relax.

Creativity

A lot of the time, it can feel like you're studying to work at a conveyor belt. Not a lot makes sense etc. However, you should never let your creative side down and develop it instead. You can develop it together with the community and me.

Guideliens and Rules

Remember to stay updated with our rules and guidelines for everyones sake.

  1. Remember that we follow the ToS of Discord
  2. Do not discriminate or be disrespectful to users.
  3. Do not attempt to spam or flood channels.
  4. Please do not attempt to create drama.
  5. Offensive avatars and names are not tolerated - will be forcefully changed and possibly banned.
  6. Please use English as your main language, as I want this server to be kept in English.
  7. Don't use alternative accounts in order to cause issues.
  8. Don’t advertise without permission from a staff member. Users with @self-promo is an exception.
  9. Don't spam mentions of roles or users.
  10. Please use the rightful channels to chat in!
  11. Do not disrespect any staff member. They're here to help!
  12. Do not ask for any staff position!
  13. Mutes, kicks and bans are entirely up to staff discretion. Arguing with staff is probably a waste of your time.
  14. Use common sense, not everything has to be written down.
  15. If you want to complain about a staff, or feel like you've been unfairly punished, contact Beebsis#0001.
