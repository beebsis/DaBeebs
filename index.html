Welcome to Da beebs

Da Beebs is a place for people to come and enjoy, while they're working or studying. Or simply if they just want to come and relax with a good atmosphere.

I want to build a Community as a place for people to come and relax, meanwhile they're studying or working. I'm am currently still a student, and I've been wanting study partners for a while to improve my own studies but it's easier said than done. So for people who're less social or have less time, I would like to give them the chance of getting a virtual study place and study partner to interactive with and increase their and my own learning.

- Beebsis 2022

Goals

These are the goals I have set for myself and the Community I wish to create.

Atmosphere

I wish to create a nice and calm atmosphere which people to enjoy and concentrate on their work.

Of course we'll have our chaotic moments from time to time.

Safe Place

A safe place for people to come and escape classmates, colleagues and just every day trouble. This isn't a venting place, but we'll listen and help if possible.

Learning

You'll see everyone learning in one way or another in here, some of us study together around the world, even if it's completely different subjects and timezones.

Acknowledgement

A lot of people don't feel acknowledged in their daily life, especially students. I wish to let them know, that they're acknowledged by us, and give them a place to enjoy.

Creativity

A lot of the time, it can feel like you're progressing to work at a conveyor belt. Not a lot makes sense etc. However, you should never let your creative side down and develop it instead. You can develop it together with the community.
